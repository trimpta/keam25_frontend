Instructions

Copy your response sheet from keam website, and paste it in the form.

  • Go to the official KEAM 2025 website and log into your account.
  • Find and open the Response Sheet section.

  • Copy all the text from your response sheet:
    On Desktop: Press Ctrl + A to select all, then Ctrl + C to copy.
    On Android: Tap and hold on any word, then choose Select All from the popup. Once everything’s highlighted, tap Copy.


  • Paste the copied text into the Response Sheet box below on this site.
  • Choose your Exam Date

  • Hit Calculate!
